## Giving Credit Where It's Due

At ESIIL we know navigating authorship agreements and recognizing team contributions is not a simple process. To help faciliate  and provide guidance on crediting contributions, all ESIIL working groups are responsible for reading our Guidlines for Intellectual Contributions and Credit. Please find these guidelines below. 

BALCH, J., Nagy, R. C., Mary Rose Posa, SULLIVAN, S., Batchelor, R., & Tuff, T. (2025). [ESIIL Guidelines for Intellectual Contributions and Credit](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18405881)
