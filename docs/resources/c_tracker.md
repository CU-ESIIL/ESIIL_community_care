## Tracking Team Contributions

Within the ESIIL Guidelines for Intellectual Contributions and Credit you will find a link to our CRediT Contributions Table. To make that easier to access we have also provided a direct copy template of that table [here](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1LTnkPWJSHXFZAVwVKQ4VYIslsqrbeuQSM3VwAlTCA1k/copy).  

You will also find a link to our [Authorship Agreement Form](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1NELo8rl6bn9s1ls-uk_3-ISSybZue0KQfAJbvX2sTd4/copy) in this same Guidelines document. The authorship agreement form is different from the tracker which helps team members log or check off what they contributed to any team product, but especially manuscripts. The Authorship Agreement Form is the criteria that the group comes to a shared agreement on in terms of significant, substantial and modest contributions. 

As a result, the CRediT Table and this Authorship Agreement Form work together to create the contribution and credit backbone of the working group.
